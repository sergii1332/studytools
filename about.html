About StudyTools

Free tools made to help students study smarter.

What We Offer

StudyTools offers a collection of free online calculators designed for students. Our tools include grade calculators, GPA calculators, percentage calculators, final grade calculators, weighted grade calculators and more.

All calculators are available directly in your browser with no registration required. Our goal is to help students save time and better manage their academic performance.

Our Goal

StudyTools provides free online calculators and study tools for students. Our goal is to make simple academic tools easy to use, fast and accessible.

Why Students Use StudyTools

Students use StudyTools to quickly calculate grades, GPA, percentages, weighted averages and exam scores without downloading software or creating an account.

Our calculators are designed to be simple, fast and accessible from any device including desktops, tablets and mobile phones.
