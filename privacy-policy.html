Privacy Policy

How StudyTools handles user privacy.

StudyTools provides free online calculators and study tools for students. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when visitors use our website.

Information We Collect

StudyTools does not require users to create an account to use the calculators. Most tools work directly in your browser and do not require you to submit personal information.

We may collect basic non-personal information through analytics tools, such as pages visited, device type, browser type and general usage data. This helps us understand how visitors use the website and improve the user experience.

Cookies and Analytics

StudyTools may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, performance measurement and website improvement. These technologies help us understand which tools are useful and how the website can be improved.

Advertising

StudyTools may use advertising services in the future to support the website and keep the tools free. Advertising providers may use cookies or similar technologies to show relevant ads.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics providers, hosting services or advertising platforms. These services may process limited data according to their own privacy policies.

Personal Information

StudyTools does not ask users to create an account or provide personal information to use the calculators. If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

Data Security

We aim to keep StudyTools simple, safe and accessible. However, no website or online service can guarantee complete security.

Children's Privacy

StudyTools is intended to provide educational tools for general student use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
