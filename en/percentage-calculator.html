Percentage Calculator







How to Use the Percentage Calculator

Enter the part value and the whole value, then click Calculate. The calculator will instantly determine what percentage the part represents of the whole.

This free percentage calculator is useful for students, teachers, businesses, discounts, statistics and everyday calculations.

Percentage Formula

The percentage is calculated by dividing the part by the whole and multiplying the result by 100.
