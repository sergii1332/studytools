How to Use the Percentage Calculator

Enter the part value and the whole value, then click Calculate. The calculator instantly determines what percentage the part represents of the whole.

Percentage Formula

Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100

Example Calculation

If 25 students out of 40 students passed an exam:

Percentage = (25 ÷ 40) × 100 = 62.5%

Therefore, 62.5% of students passed the exam.

What Is a Percentage?

A percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. Percentages are commonly used in education, business, finance, statistics and everyday calculations.

Common Uses for Percentages

  • Exam and test scores
  • Discount calculations
  • Sales tax calculations
  • Financial analysis
  • Business reports
  • Statistical data
