How to Use the Final Grade Calculator

Enter your current grade, the weight of your final exam and your desired final grade. The calculator will show the score you need on the final exam.

Final Grade Formula

Required Final Exam Score = (Desired Grade - Current Grade × Current Weight) ÷ Final Exam Weight

Example Calculation

If your current grade is 80%, your final exam is worth 20% and you want a final course grade of 85%, the calculator finds the score you need on the final exam.

Required Score = (85 - 80 × 0.80) ÷ 0.20 = 105%

In this example, you would need 105% on the final exam, which means the desired final grade may not be realistic unless extra credit is available.

Why Use a Final Grade Calculator?

This calculator helps students understand how much their final exam affects their overall grade. It is useful when planning study time, setting target grades or checking whether a desired grade is still possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does final exam weight mean?

Final exam weight is the percentage of your total course grade that comes from your final exam.

Can this calculator tell me if I can still pass?

Yes. Enter the grade you need to pass as your desired final grade and the calculator will show the exam score required.

What if the required score is over 100%?

A required score above 100% usually means your target grade is not possible without extra credit or grade adjustments.
