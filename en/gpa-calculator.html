How to Use the GPA Calculator

Enter the grade points for each class and click Calculate GPA. The calculator will automatically compute your average GPA.

This free GPA calculator uses the standard 4.0 GPA scale and is useful for high school, college and university students.

What Is GPA?

GPA stands for Grade Point Average. It is a common academic measurement used by schools, colleges and universities to evaluate student performance.
