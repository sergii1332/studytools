How to Use the GPA Calculator

Enter the grade points for each course and click Calculate GPA. The calculator will automatically compute your average Grade Point Average.

What Is GPA?

GPA stands for Grade Point Average. It is one of the most common academic measurements used by schools, colleges and universities to evaluate student performance.

GPA Formula

GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Number of Courses

Example GPA Calculation

Suppose you received the following grade points:

  • Course 1: 4.0
  • Course 2: 3.5
  • Course 3: 3.0
  • Course 4: 4.0

GPA = (4.0 + 3.5 + 3.0 + 4.0) ÷ 4 = 3.63

Why GPA Matters

GPA is often used for college admissions, scholarships, academic awards and graduation requirements. Maintaining a strong GPA can improve future educational and career opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good GPA?

A GPA above 3.0 is generally considered good, while a GPA above 3.5 is often considered excellent.

Does this calculator use a 4.0 scale?

Yes. This GPA calculator is designed for the standard 4.0 GPA scale.

Can I calculate college and high school GPA?

Yes. The calculator can be used for both high school and college courses.
