How to Use the Weighted Grade Calculator

Enter each grade and its corresponding weight percentage. The calculator will automatically compute your weighted average grade.

This free weighted grade calculator is useful for students whose courses use different percentages for assignments, projects, quizzes and exams.

What Is a Weighted Grade?

A weighted grade gives different importance to different assignments. For example, a final exam may count for 40% of the course grade while homework counts for 20%.

Weighted Grade Formula

A weighted average is calculated by multiplying each grade by its weight, adding the results together and dividing by the total weight.
