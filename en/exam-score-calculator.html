How to Use the Exam Score Calculator

Enter the number of correct answers and the total number of questions, then click Calculate. The calculator will show your exam percentage.

This free exam score calculator is useful for students and teachers who want to quickly calculate a test score or percentage grade.

Exam Score Formula

The exam score is calculated by dividing the number of correct answers by the total number of questions and multiplying the result by 100.
