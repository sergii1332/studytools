How to Use the Exam Score Calculator

Enter the number of correct answers and the total number of questions. Click Calculate to instantly determine your exam percentage.

Exam Score Formula

Exam Score (%) = (Correct Answers ÷ Total Questions) × 100

Example Calculation

If you answered 42 questions correctly out of 50 questions:

(42 ÷ 50) × 100 = 84%

Your exam score would be 84%.

Why Use an Exam Score Calculator?

Students use exam score calculators to quickly determine their grades after a test. Teachers can also use them to calculate percentage scores for quizzes, exams and classroom assessments.
