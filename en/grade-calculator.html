How to Use the Grade Calculator

Enter all your grades and click Calculate. The calculator will instantly determine your average grade based on the values provided.

Grade Calculator Formula

Average Grade = Sum of All Grades ÷ Number of Grades

Example Calculation

Suppose your grades are:

  • 85
  • 90
  • 78
  • 92

Average Grade = (85 + 90 + 78 + 92) ÷ 4 = 86.25

What Is a Grade Calculator?

A grade calculator helps students determine their average academic performance. It is commonly used to track progress throughout a course, estimate report card grades and prepare for final exams.

Why Students Use Grade Calculators

Students use grade calculators to monitor academic performance, identify areas for improvement and estimate future grades before important exams or assignments.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my average grade?

Add all your grades together and divide the total by the number of grades.

Can I use this for college grades?

Yes. This calculator works for high school, college and university grades.
